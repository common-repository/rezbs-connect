RezBS Connect connects Wordpress to the RezBS booking platform for online booking integration.

== Description ==

**RezBS** is the primary tour booking and management platform for The Bahamas. The platform is a cloud-based software as a service booking and management system for tour operators throughout the Bahamas. The platform is the **only** one that allows tour operators to take credit card payments tied directly to their own e-commerce accounts with all major Bahamian banks.

Details can be found at [RezBS.com](https://rezbs.com/). 

RezBS Connect if completely free to use, and connects your Wordpress website to your RezBS account to allow simple insertion of tour and trip booking links through shortcodes.

Buttons can be customised with any label or class, and inserted into any page on your site quickly and simply.

RezBS Connect is compatible with Classic Editor, Gutenberg, and most pagebuilders (tested in Beaver Builder, Elementor, Divi & WP Bakery).

####Requirements:

* An account on the RezBS booking platform.
* an SSL certificate installed on your site for security.
* the API key found in your RezBS dashbaord under [integration](https://rezbs.com/integrate_wp.php).

####Support for RezBS

If you need assistance getting your account, or this plug-in set up, please use the Support tab in your RezBS account.


== Installation ==

1. Install the ResBS Connect plugin
2. Activate the plugin
3. Enter your RezBS.com API key under the RezBS Connect menu (this can be found under "Integrations" in your account.)
4. Add your Book Now buttons using the text editor or your preferred page builder.
